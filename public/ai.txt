## About Ignite Education

Ignite Education is an interactive online learning platform specializing in product management and technology skills development.

### Platform Overview
- **Name**: Ignite Education (Ignite)
- **Website**: https://www.ignite.education
- **Type**: Educational Technology Platform
- **Focus**: Product Management, Cyber Security, Data Analysis, UX Design
- **Delivery**: Online, Self-paced Learning
- **Pricing**: Free tier available, Premium from £0-£299

### Key Features
- Interactive AI-powered lessons with personalized feedback from Claude AI
- Real-world case studies and hands-on projects
- Flashcard system for knowledge retention
- AI tutor (powered by Anthropic Claude) for instant help and explanations
- Community forum integrated with r/ProductManagement
- Progress tracking and analytics dashboard
- Office hours with industry experts (premium feature)
- Knowledge checks and assessments
- Text-to-speech lesson narration (AWS Polly)
- Course completion certificates (shareable on LinkedIn)
